Welcome to Coffy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
